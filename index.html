Who We Are

At Intermar, we are committed to redefining freight forwarding and logistics services through expertise and innovation. With a dedicated team of high-caliber professionals, we provide end-to-end transportation solutions tailored to your business needs. Whether it’s air, sea, or land, our mission is to make global shipping effortless for you.

  • Extensive global network covering all continents.
  • Customized logistics solutions for businesses of all sizes.
  • Trusted by businesses for efficient and timely deliveries.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative and reliable logistics solutions, connecting Egypt to the world with seamless, efficient, and cost-effective shipping services. We aim to redefine the logistics landscape through advanced technology, exceptional customer experiences, and sustainable practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses and individuals by delivering tailored logistics solutions that simplify the complexities of global trade. Through our expertise, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and dedicated team, we ensure timely, secure, and affordable shipping services. We are committed to fostering strong relationships, driving growth, and contributing to the success of our clients and partners.
